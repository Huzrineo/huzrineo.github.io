## ⚙️ Using ForgeFlow

Once you have installed ForgeFlow, you can start creating automated workflows.

1. **Open ForgeFlow.** Look for the ForgeFlow icon on your desktop or applications folder.
2. **Create a New Workflow.** Click on the "New Workflow" button on the main screen.
3. **Add Tasks.** Choose actions from the list. You can add tasks such as opening applications, sending emails, or organizing files.
4. **Save Your Workflow.** Give your workflow a name and save it to use later.
5. **Run Your Workflow.** Select your saved workflow and click "Run" to start automating your tasks.
